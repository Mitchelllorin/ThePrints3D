Data Safety

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Package: com.theprints3d.app

✓ No data collected or shared

ThePrints3D runs entirely in the browser and on your device. Your architectural drawings are never uploaded. No account required, no analytics, no tracking.

Data Collection Summary

Category Collected? Shared? Purpose
Personal info (name, email, etc.) No No —
Drawing / floor plan files No No Processed locally on your device using WASM/WebGPU. Never uploaded.
Project data Yes (local only) No Saved to device IndexedDB so your projects persist between sessions
Location No No —
App activity / analytics No No —
Device identifiers No No —

Data Sharing

ThePrints3D does not share any data with third parties. There are no advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers involved in this app.

Security Practices

All processing — including AI wall detection (U-Net ONNX model via WebGPU/WASM), PDF rendering, and 3D model generation — happens locally on your device. No network requests are made to external servers during normal operation.

Android Permissions

INTERNET

Required by the Capacitor app container to load the web view correctly. No external network requests are made to our servers during normal use.

READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+)

Requested only when you choose to import a floor plan image from your device's media library. Not required to use the app.

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android ≤12)

Requested only when importing files on older Android versions. No files are uploaded or transmitted.

Data Deletion

Since no data is stored on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete on our end. To clear locally saved projects, uninstall the app or go to Android Settings → Apps → ThePrints3D → Clear Data.
