Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

ThePrints3D ("the App") converts architectural drawing files into interactive 3D models. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Drawing files: PDF and image files you upload are processed entirely on-device. They are never transmitted to any external server.

Project data: Projects you save are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. No account or sign-in is required, and no project data leaves your device.

Usage analytics: The App does not collect analytics, crash reports, or any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

Permissions

Internet: Required for the app to load correctly inside its app container. No outbound network requests to external servers are made during normal use.

Storage (Android 13+): Read access to media files is requested only when you choose to import images from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and in the app-store listings.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at privacy@theprints3d.com.
