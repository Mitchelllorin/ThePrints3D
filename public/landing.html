How it works

Three steps to 3D

From raw floor-plan file to interactive 3D model in under a minute.

1

Upload your drawings

Drag and drop PDF or image files — entire drawing sets are supported. ThePrints3D rasterises each sheet and extracts scale information from the title block automatically.

2

AI detects the structure

A lightweight U-Net neural network (trained on CubiCasa5k) segments walls, doors, and windows from each sheet via WebGPU or WASM. A heuristic edge detector serves as fallback — so it always works, even without the model.

3

Explore in 3D

Orbit, zoom, and pan through your building. Toggle layer visibility, measure distances, drop annotation pins, place products, and export a PNG snapshot to share with your team.

Features

Everything you need on site

Designed for architects, contractors, and estimators who work with floor plans every day.

📐

Drag-and-drop upload

Accepts PDF, PNG, JPG, TIFF, and WebP. Entire drawing sets — multiple sheets, multiple floors — processed in one go.

🤖

AI wall detection

U-Net ONNX model runs entirely on-device via WebGPU or WASM. Heuristic edge detector kicks in automatically when the model file is not present — it always works.

AI-powered
🏗

Interactive 3D viewer

Three.js canvas with orbit / pan / zoom, perspective and orthographic camera presets, shadow casting, and a live performance stats overlay.

🔧

Layer toggles

Show or hide walls, doors, windows, and rooms independently to focus on what matters for each review.

📏

Distance measurement

Click any two points on the 3D model surface to get an accurate real-world distance in metres or feet. Saved to a collapsible panel.

📌

Annotation pins

Place labelled, colour-coded pins anywhere on the model. Perfect for marking punch-list items, design notes, or site observations.

🛋

Product placement

Drop doors, windows, plumbing fixtures, HVAC units, lighting, and flooring into the scene from a built-in product catalogue.

📊

Material estimator

Auto-calculates studs, plates, drywall sheets, insulation area, masonry volume, and CMU block count from the detected walls.

🔢

Unit converter & calculators

Imperial ↔ metric conversions and common construction calculators — available from the Toolbox even before a model is built.

💾

Local project library

Save projects to browser IndexedDB and reopen them later — no account required, no cloud sync, no data leaving your device.

📤

Share PNG snapshot

Export the current 3D view as a high-resolution PNG image you can paste into reports, emails, or project management tools.

📱

PWA & mobile-friendly

Installable as a Progressive Web App on desktop and mobile. Works offline after first load. Android APK also available via Capacitor 8.

Offline-ready
🔒

Your drawings never leave your device

ThePrints3D runs entirely on-device. There is no backend server, no file upload, no telemetry. All processing — including AI inference — happens locally using WebAssembly and WebGPU. Your architectural drawings stay yours.

Tech stack

Modern, open-source tools

No proprietary dependencies. Built entirely on widely-used, battle-tested open-source libraries.
